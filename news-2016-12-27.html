Artificial Graphite Optimization Makes Lithium Battery Has Higher Capacity And Longer Cycle Life

December 27, 2016

      Lithium battery with higher capacity and longer cycle life are the same targets of lithium battery manufacturers and customers, but there are many materials, which would be the right and safety choice? In the GGLB meeting of this year, SDK showed there new material, which brought a good choice to lithium battery manufactures.

      Yasuaki Wakizaka from SDK made presentation to their new products: SCMGTMCF-C and XR-FC. SCMGTM is the technology accumulation of SDK’s Omachi manufacture base, this manufacture base was built 8 yeas ago. Meanwhile, Yasuaki Wakizaka showed the charging&discharging rate diagram, cycle life, capacity of these two material, compared with hard carbon.

      On the same condition, SCMGTMCF-C’s energy density is 23% higher than hard carbon, has higher charging&discharging rate, and gives battery a better cycle life. SCMGTM XR-FC has similar performance with natural graphite, but has better cycle life and lower internal resistance.
