Lithium Battery Price May Rise In 2017 Because Of Cobalt Salt Shortage

December 29, 2016

      Since the beginning of July,the new energy vehicles policy gradually landed, cobalt industry supply and demand situation continues to ferment: domestic and international cobalt metal prices continued to rise; Cobalt oxide (II,III) and cobalt sulfate price have risen more than 37%.

      On the supply side, overseas cobalt ore output fell, and the continue of Congo political crisis exacerbated the situation. On the other hand, domestic raw materials have been consumed. In this context, overseas cobalt prices keep rising, which makes the market price of cobalt is expected, smelting enterprises hold cobalt salt waiting for better prices.

      On the demand side, overseas cobalt metal smelting enterprises cut their output, resulting in imbalance between supply and demand of cobalt. Domestic demand for consumer electronics risen in third&fourth quarter, which makes the need of cobalt volume increased. Coupled with material enterprises hoarding cobalt, makes the domestic price of cobalt salt keeps rising.

      In the short term, the price of cobalt will remain unchanged. The medium-term price will depend on the political situation in the Congo. If the political situation becomes stable, the cobalt price will mainly depend on battery material demand and supply situation. But if the Congo political keeps unstable, it is still only a starting point, cobalt price is unpredictable.

      Even assuming the Congo political became stable , there will be more than 2,000 tons of supply and demand gap in 2017, this is the first time in recent years, and will expand year by year. We are optimistic about the long-term trend of cobalt prices, and there is possibility of a surge in the participation of social funds.
