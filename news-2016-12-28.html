China's Lithium Battery Industry Shows Advantages In International Competition

December 28, 2016

      Japan is the leading country in lithium battery industry during the last 20 years, but China and Korea are rising to challenge the leading position of Japan. Based on the biggest material supply basement, the biggest E-car market, and the great support of capital&government, China has significant advantages in this competition.

      Japanese lithium battery market expert Sachiya has researched China&Japan lithium market for years, he compared the competitive strategies of Japan&China, summarized that the advantages of Japan’s lithium industry are the concentration of Industrial and the advancement of technology. Correspond, China’s lithium industry got capital, market, and scale advantages.

      Meanwhile, Japan&China’s lithium industry has respective advantages. Japan’s enterprise didn’t put core technology into China, worried about the technology leaking. Besides, Japan’s lithium enterprise has a lack of communication with China’s local supplier, which makes their lithium battery price is high, could not occupy the low-end market. China’s lithium enterprise need to improve their R&D strength and strengthen industry communication.

      Sachiya Inagaki though that China is the most likely to become the leading country in lithium battery market, due to the significant support of capital&government, and the increasing of China’s local market. And there is no doubt that the competition among Japan, China, Korea will become increasingly fierce.

      Although China’s lithium technology could not compare with Japan&Korea, but China’s lithium enterprises can get more market share with cost advantages, corresponding, Japan’s lithium enterprises will lose market shares. Meanwhile, Korea’s lithium enterprises are became dependent on China’s anode material supply, which makes they are going to lose China’s lithium market share.
